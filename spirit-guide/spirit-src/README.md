# Spirit Source

Raw source documents that establish what this product does and why it exists.

## What Goes Here

Drop in the original, unprocessed documents that inform the project. These are the inputs — not decisions, but the raw material decisions are made from.

Examples:
- Company overviews and positioning docs
- Client decks, meeting notes, proposals
- Methodology papers and technical process docs
- Product one-pagers and marketing materials
- Academic papers, interview transcripts, industry research

## Naming Convention

All files in this folder use a prefix to classify their type:
- `CLIENT_` — client-facing materials (decks, meeting notes, proposals)
- `COMPANY_` — internal company docs (overviews, positioning)
- `METHOD_` — methodology and technical process
- `PRODUCT_` — product descriptions and marketing
- `RESEARCH_` — papers, interviews, industry research

Keep the original filename after the prefix for traceability. Example: `quarterly-deck-pantheon.pdf` → `CLIENT_quarterly-deck-pantheon.pdf`
